# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Single user (the developer). Daily context: quick morning or end-of-day check-in to see spending, balances, and any anomalies. Occasional deeper sessions for monthly budget planning and invoice reconciliation.

## Product Purpose

Personal finance control app migrated from Python/Streamlit to Spring Boot. Tracks spending, manages budgets, monitors credit card invoices, and visualizes financial health. Success means a 30-second daily glance tells you everything you need to know — no hunting, no heavy forms.

## Brand Personality

Clean, technical, dark, professional. No-nonsense and data-focused. Portuguese UI with a developer-friendly feel — not a bank, not a fintech startup.

## Anti-references

Not like traditional bank apps. Avoid heavy, cluttered bank UIs — dense tables, too many fields, hierarchical menus, formal financial tones.

## Design Principles

1. **Dark by default** — GitHub-inspired dark theme is the identity, not an afterthought. Atmosphere sets the mood for financial reflection.
2. **Data first** — charts and numbers lead; decoration follows. Every pixel either carries information or gets out of the way.
3. **Fast glances** — designed for daily 30-second check-ins. Surface what matters (today's spending, balance) immediately; bury configuration.
4. **No bank clutter** — avoid dense forms, heavy tables, and old-school financial UI patterns. Keep it lean and scannable.
5. **Tool not toy** — clean and technical over polished and fancy. Professional restraint beats visual flair.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard web best practices. No specific WCAG level target beyond what Bootstrap's dark theme provides. Reasonable contrast, semantic HTML, keyboard navigation where it matters.
